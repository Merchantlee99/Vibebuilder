# Quality Rubric

## Plan Quality

- Goal is implementation-ready.
- Scope and non-goals are explicit.
- Validation covers normal path and at least one failure or edge path for `normal+`.
- Rollback or recovery is concrete.
- High-risk work includes red-team or risk review.

## Review Quality

- Reviewer is independent from producer.
- Findings are evidence-based.
- Validation evidence is reviewed, not only mentioned.
- Residual risk is explicit.

## Validation Evidence

- Command or check is named.
- Result is recorded.
- If a check was not run, the reason is recorded.
