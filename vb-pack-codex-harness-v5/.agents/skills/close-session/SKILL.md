# Close Session

Use this before final completion.

## Steps

1. Confirm the result matches the plan and acceptance criteria.
2. Run or report deterministic validation.
3. For `normal+`, ensure independent review exists or clearly report why it could not be completed.
4. Record known gaps and residual risks.
5. Decide whether follow-up automation is needed.
6. Keep the final answer concise and outcome-focused.

## Non-Trigger

Do not use for early brainstorming or partial progress updates.

## Completion Shape

Return:

- outcome
- validation performed
- review status
- risks or gaps
- follow-up, if any
